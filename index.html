1. Capybara

Capybaras are one of the most chill animals on the planet, even allowing small birds
on top of them at times. Kinda expensive to keep as an actual pet though.

2. Dog

Man's best friend. Almost everyone has one. With many different breeds of dog,
you can't go wrong with having a dog or two.

3. Rabbit

Rabbits can actually be kept as pets, and they are very cute and hyper at times.
Fun fact: Carrots are actually harmful to rabbits. They actually prefer wheat.

4. Owl

A nighttime predator, they mainly hunt small prey such as rats, rabbits, and even other birds.
Owls can also turn their heads 180 degrees.

5. Bees

The most important insect on the planet, these little guys can boast on keeping entire ecosystems alive.
Flowering plants need bees in order to survive, and these plants produce fruits and vegetables for other animals to eat.
